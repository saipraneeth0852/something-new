# Product Requirements Document: StatusHub India ## Target Audience - **Demographic:** Indian users (specifically older "uncle/aunt" demographic). - **UX Principles:** Big buttons, simple navigation, Hindi/Telugu support, one-click actions. No complex tools or hidden features. ## Core Features & Screens ### 1. Splash Screen - **Purpose:** Branding + preload APIs. - **UI:** App logo (center), Tagline: “Daily Status & Wallpapers”, simple gradient background. - **Duration:** 1.5–2 sec. ### 2. Home Screen (Priority) - **Layout:** App Name, Big Card (Status Maker ⭐), Wallpapers, Quotes, Banner Ad. - **UX:** Big buttons, clear icons + text. Status Maker must be highlighted. ### 3. Wallpapers Section - **Categories:** God 🙏, Nature 🌿, Motivation 💪, Festivals 🎉. - **Grid View:** 2-column image grid. - **Full Screen View:** Full image with Download and Share buttons. Swipe left/right navigation. ### 4. Quotes Section (Reels Style) - **UI:** Full-screen vertical swipe. - **Content:** Large text quotes (e.g., "भगवान पर भरोसा रखो 🙏"). - **Actions:** Copy 📋, Share 📤. ### 5. Status Maker (Core Feature 🔥) - **Flow:** 1. **Category Selection:** Bhakti 🙏, Good Morning 🌅, Festivals 🎉, General 💬. 2. **Template Selection:** 6–10 simple templates. 3. **Preview Screen:** Displays generated image (quote + template) with Download, Share to WhatsApp, and "Regenerate" 🔄 buttons. ### 6. Daily Notification Flow - **Message:** “Good Morning 🙏 Your status is ready”. - **Action:** Tap directly opens the Preview screen in Status Maker. ### 7. Monetization (Ad Placement) - **Home:** Banner ad. - **Wallpapers:** Grid + Interstitial after 4-5 opens. - **Status Download:** Interstitial before/after download. - **Rule:** Never interrupt the creation flow. ## Design Style - **Theme:** Premium Dark Theme. - **Visuals:** High contrast, clear typography, intuitive icons.
